Manage your projects

Create Projects

With this website you can manage all your projects. Simply create an account and you can start creating your first project.

Using the simple form you can create your project. All you need is a name for your project.

Someone else already created the project? Click the join button for your project and you can access all its contents.

Manage Sprints and Stories

Create a sprint by filling out the form. All you need is a name, start date and end date.

To create an overview of what stories you want to do, you can add stories to the sprint by clicking on the 'Add Stories' button.

You can manage your progress by dragging your stories on the board. Doing this updates your Burndown Chart

Define Roles

You can define member roles on the Project Dashboard. This way you know what each members expertise is.

These roles have no extra value. It's just for your own benefit. To have a nice overview of all the members in your project.

To edit your roles, simply click on the Edit button on the Project Dashboard.
